Chicken Adobo Recipe

Chicken adobo is a classic Filipino dish featuring tender chicken marinated and simmered in a savory blend of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices. It's simple yet flavorful, perfect for a comforting meal. This recipe serves 4.

Ingredients

2 lbs (about 1 kg) chicken thighs or drumsticks (bone-in for best flavor)
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup vinegar (white or apple cider)
1 head garlic, minced (about 10-12 cloves)
2 bay leaves
1 tsp whole peppercorns
1 cup water
2 tbsp cooking oil
Salt and pepper to taste (optional)
Optional: 1-2 tbsp brown sugar

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Combine chicken, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns. Let marinate for 30 mins to 2 hours.
  2. Cook the Chicken: Heat oil, brown chicken, then add the marinade and water. Simmer for 25â€“30 mins.
  3. Adjust and Serve: Add salt, pepper, or sugar as desired. Serve with rice.

Tips

For a twist, try adding potatoes or hard-boiled eggs during simmering. This dish tastes even better the next day!

Enjoy your homemade Chicken Adobo!
