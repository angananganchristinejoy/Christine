Kare-Kare Recipe!

Join the fun in making this creamy, peanutty Filipino stew! Perfect for cozy family dinners. Serves 4-6 hungry adventurers. Prep time: 20 mins | Cook time: 2 hours

Magical Ingredients

  • 2 lbs (900g) oxtail or beef shank, cut into pieces
  • 1 lb (450g) beef tripe
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup ground toasted peanuts
  • 2 tbsp annatto powder
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 eggplants, sliced
  • 1 bunch string beans
  • 1 bunch bok choy
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce (patis)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Bagoong for serving

Step-by-Step Cooking Quest

Step 1: Tenderize the Meats!
In a large pot, boil the oxtail (and tripe) for 1.5-2 hours until tender. Save the broth.
Step 2: SautÃ© the Aromatics!

Step 3: Add the Color Magic!
Add annatto and meats, pour broth, and simmer.
Step 4: Peanut Power-Up!
Mix peanut butter with warm broth, then add in. Season and simmer.
Step 5: Veggie Party Time!
Add eggplant and beans, cook 5 mins. Add bok choy.
Step 6: Serve with Love!
Serve over rice with bagoong.
